About

Warm, personal, and present—think of me as the bridesmaid who always has a plan.

Meet Danielle

Hi! I’m Danielle—the planner behind The Borrowed Bridesmaid. I’m based in Dallas, PA, and I created this business because I believe your wedding day should be filled with joy, presence, and the people you love… not logistics.

I fell in love with planning while organizing my own wedding and quickly realized how much happiness I get from helping others bring their vision to life. I’m detail-obsessed in the best possible way and truly enjoy getting to know each couple—learning what matters most to them, what inspires them, and how they want their day to feel. My favorite part of this work is transforming all of that into a celebration that feels deeply personal and unforgettable.

I work with couples throughout Northeast Pennsylvania and beyond, up to three hours from Scranton—including Philadelphia and New York City. Whether you’re looking for full-service planning, day-of coordination, or help with all the pre-wedding festivities (like showers, bachelorette weekends, or your rehearsal dinner), I’m here to make the entire process smooth, stress-free, and enjoyable.

My goal?
That you wake up on your wedding day feeling calm, excited, and completely cared for—because you deserve to be fully present for every moment you’ll remember for the rest of your life.
